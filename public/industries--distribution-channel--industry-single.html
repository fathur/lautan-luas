Water Treatment

We serve to almost all segment of water chemical business in Indonesia such as raw and waste water treatment, boiler & cooling treatment, oil & gas, mining, sugar and others. We have highly experience water treatment team and laboratory facilities all across most big cities in Indonesia serving some 1,000 industrial end-users throughout Indonesia and the Asia-Pacific region.
